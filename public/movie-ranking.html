The Best Movies According to Bikram I have ever seen

My top 3 BEngali movies of all-time.

Pather Panchali (Song of the Little Road)

Directed by Satyajit Ray, this 1955 film is the first installment in the renowned "Apu Trilogy." It's widely considered a masterpiece of Indian cinema and is celebrated for its poignant portrayal of rural life in Bengal.

Devi (The Goddess)

Directed by Satyajit Ray, this 1960 film explores themes of superstition, religious fervor, and the exploitation of women. It's praised for its powerful storytelling and thought-provoking commentary on societal issues.

Nayak (The Hero)

Directed by Satyajit Ray, this 1966 film follows the journey of a film star during a train journey where he confronts his past, present, and future. It's admired for its insightful character study and exploration of fame, identity, and existential angst.
